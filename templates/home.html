Frequently Asked Questions

What is the largest cabin you have?

Mohave Cabin with Treehouse is our largest, sleeping up to 33 guests across 7 bedrooms plus the treehouse. It's ideal for big family reunions, large extended-family gatherings, and group events.

Where exactly are the cabins located?

Both Parkway Lodge and Mohave Cabin are located just west of Rainbow Lake in Lakeside, Arizona — part of the Pinetop-Lakeside community in the White Mountains. It's about 3.5 hours from Phoenix via the US-60 or AZ-260.

Can we book both cabins at the same time?

Yes — if you need space for up to 60 people, booking both cabins together is an option. Call us to discuss availability and group pricing.

Are the cabins good for kids?

Absolutely. Rainbow Lake is safe for kids to fish and explore, the Mohave Cabin treehouse is a huge hit with children, and the forested setting is perfect for outdoor adventures of all kinds.
