# Keyword Research — Arizona Family Cabins
*Source: Ubersuggest, pulled 2026-06-26. Volumes are monthly US searches. "Comp" = paid competition 0–1 (proxy for commercial intensity).*

## The strategy in one sentence
Win the **Pinetop-Lakeside + Show Low "cabin rental" searches** and the **"large group / family reunion" searches** with dedicated pages, then capture **top-of-funnel planning traffic** ("things to do in Pinetop", "family reunion ideas") with blog guides that funnel readers to the two cabins — building toward **direct bookings** instead of Airbnb/VRBO.

## Key insight: strong summer seasonality
"Cabins in Pinetop AZ" runs **2,900/mo in Jun–Jul** but drops to **1,600/mo in winter**. Publish and promote the money pages by **early spring** so they're indexed and ranking before the summer booking rush.
